## CLIENT IMPORT — All 86 Columns

### A. Client Name

PLEASE DELETE THIS ROW BEFORE IMPORTING The three pink columns below are mandatory. You can complete the rest in the app later.  The system does not allow duplicates on import,  if you have duplicate client names, please email support@mydatagrows.com so we can remove this rule from your database.

### B. Status

Please select Active, Inactive, Pending or Dormant from the dropdown list.  Active and Dormant clients will continue to schedule tasks. Inactive and pending clients won't.

### AA. Partner

Add your partners, managers and accountants under the Accountant column on the Tips & Formats sheet and a dropdown will be created.

### AB. Manager

Add your partners, managers and accountants under the Accountant column on the Tips & Formats sheet and a dropdown will be created.

### AC. Accountant

Add your accountants under the Accountant column on the Tips & Formats sheet and a dropdown will be created.  Staff added here will be assigned as the accountant for each task for the client, unless one of the other roles to the right is chosen.

### AD. Accounting Role

Only needed if the accounting person is different from the Accountant column.   The Accounting Role will override the accountant for this specific department. If they are the same, leave the column blank.

### AE. CIPC Role

Only needed if the CIPC person is different from the Accountant column.   The CIPC Role will override the accountant for the CIPC department. If they are the same, leave the column blank.

### AF. Financials Role

Only needed if the Financials and Tax person is different from the Accountant column.   The Financials Role will override the accountant for the financials department. If they are the same, leave the column blank.

### AG. HR Role

Only needed if the HR person is different from the Accountant column.   The HR Role will override the accountant for the Payroll department. If they are the same, leave the column blank.

### AH. Tax Role

Only needed if the Tax person is different from the Accountant column.   The Tax Role will override the accountant for the Tax department. If they are the same, leave the column blank.

### AM. VAT Type

Select from the dropdown: Monthly: Every month Even: Periods of two months ending on the last day of February, April, June, August, October and December. Due date on the last day of the following month. Eg: Period January 2022 and February 2022, Due date 31 March. Odd: Periods of two months ending on the last day of January, March, May, July, September, November. Due date on the last day of the following month. Eg: Period February 2022 and March 2022, Due date 31 April. Bi-Annual: Twice a year
